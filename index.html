Crawlspace Encapsulation & Waterproofing

Moisture in the crawlspace can lead to mold, wood rot, high humidity, and expensive structural damage. We provide complete crawlspace encapsulation and waterproofing solutions to keep your home healthy, dry, and energy efficient.

  • Professional vapor barrier and full encapsulation systems
  • Drainage and sump pump solutions for standing water
  • Dehumidifiers and long-term moisture control
  • Protection against mold growth and wood decay
  • Improved indoor air quality and energy efficiency
