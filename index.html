Crawlspace Encapsulation & Waterproofing

Moisture in the crawlspace can lead to mold, wood rot, high humidity, and expensive structural damage. Eades Plumbing and Heating provides complete crawlspace encapsulation and waterproofing solutions to keep your home healthy, dry, and energy efficient.

  • Professional vapor barrier and encapsulation system installation
  • Drainage and sump pump solutions for standing water
  • Dehumidifiers and long-term moisture control
  • Protection against mold growth and wood decay
  • Improved indoor air quality and energy efficiency
